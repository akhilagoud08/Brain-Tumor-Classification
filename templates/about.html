About the Project

This project follows a classic medical image classification workflow: image loading, preprocessing, feature extraction, supervised model training, model comparison, and web-based prediction.

1. Kaggle MRI images are organized into Tumor and No Tumor folders.
2. Images are converted to grayscale, resized to 200x200, and normalized.
3. Raw pixels, HOG, and LBP features are extracted.
4. Six machine learning models are trained and evaluated on an 80/20 split.
5. The best-performing model is saved with Pickle and used by Flask.
